Alcazaba: The taifa of southern Spain have long impoverished since their heydays. The fortresses of the lords still stand strong, but their resources are limited. Competition over what remains is fierce. Fortunately, the relatively properous trade lines on the region's rivers provide some additional income. The map features a start with fortified walls, but below-standard starting resources. small golds are scattered around the region. Stone and shore fish are in the central desert. The large woodlines are at the hills on the edges of the map. Each player starts with a dock and trade cog and a neutral dock to trade with, protected by aqueduct walls.
